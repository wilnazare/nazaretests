What is COM-B?

COM-B is a simple way to think about behaviour change. It says a behaviour is more likely when people have the capability, opportunity, and motivation to do it.

  • Capability: do people know how, have the skill, and feel able to do it?
  • Opportunity: do the environment, workflow, resources, time, and social context enable it?
  • Motivation: do people value it, prioritise it, and intend to do it?
