# Skills Sunburst Curation v1.1

Updated the phrase grouping to be stricter about word-search noise and role-title fragments.

Notable changes:
- Removed job-title fragments such as learning designer, instructional designer, project manager, LMS administrator, and data analyst where they were being treated as skills.
- Removed generic or context-only fragments such as global learning, digital tools, management systems, technical data, strategic mind, time zones, and led training.
- Moved digital learning platforms into the learning platform ecosystem instead of digital learning production.
- Moved market insight and skills-gap language into Insight & skills analysis instead of performance consulting.
- Tightened production to digital-learning, elearning, courseware, media, and digital-experience phrases.

Counts still use the same de-duplicated 100-JD corpus baseline.
